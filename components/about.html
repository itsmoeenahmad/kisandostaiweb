About Us

Built by Students,
For the Nation.

We are three friends from the Department of Computer Science, University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Peshawar, Pakistan: Moeen Ahmed, Abdul Hannan, and Waqas Rashid. Under the supervision of Mr. Amer Taj, we created KisanDostAI as our final year project.

KisanDostAI is Pakistan's first voice-based agricultural AI system that works through a simple phone call. Farmers do not need apps, websites, or internet access. The AI listens in Urdu and provides instant farming advice using real data.
