Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

1. Information Collection

KisanDostAI collects minimal information necessary to provide voice-based agricultural assistance. When you call our service, we may record:

  • Voice queries (temporarily stored for processing)
  • Call duration and timestamp
  • Phone number (for service improvement purposes)
  • General location data (to provide region-specific advice)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Process and respond to your agricultural queries
  • Improve AI accuracy and service quality
  • Provide region-specific weather and crop information
  • Conduct research for agricultural development
  • Maintain service statistics and analytics

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Voice recordings are encrypted and automatically deleted after 30 days unless required for service improvement. Your personal information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. No Sale of Personal Data

KisanDostAI is an academic project developed at University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Peshawar, Pakistan. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your call records
  • Access information we have collected about you
  • Opt-out of data collection (service may be limited)
  • Update or correct your information

6. Children's Privacy

KisanDostAI is designed for adult farmers and agricultural workers. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
Email: support@kisandostai.com
