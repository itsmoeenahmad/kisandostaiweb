Frequently Asked Questions

Is KisanDostAI free to use?
Yes, KisanDostAI is completely free to use for all Pakistani farmers. As a university research project developed at University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Peshawar, Pakistan, our primary goal is to support the farming community with accessible agricultural information. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or premium plans. Simply call the dedicated number and start receiving instant farming guidance in Urdu at no cost.
Do I need the internet or a smartphone?
No internet connection or smartphone is required. KisanDostAI works with any basic mobile phone (even old button phones) through a simple voice call using the regular GSM network. This design ensures that even farmers in remote areas with limited connectivity can access agricultural guidance. Just dial our number like any regular phone call, and you'll be connected to the AI assistant without needing data packages, apps, or internet access.
What languages does it support?
Currently, KisanDostAI is fully optimized for Urdu language, which is widely understood across Pakistan. The AI can understand various Urdu dialects and conversational styles to accommodate farmers from different regions. We are actively working on adding support for Pashto and Punjabi languages in future updates to make the service accessible to even more farmers in their native languages. Regional language support is a priority in our development roadmap.
What can farmers ask?
Farmers can ask about a wide range of agricultural topics including crop planning strategies, real-time weather forecasts, soil health assessment and improvement techniques, pest control methods and prevention, fertilizer usage recommendations based on crop type, and advanced agriculture-related information. The AI can also provide guidance on best planting times, crop rotation practices, water management, disease identification, and market price trends for various crops across Pakistan.
Is it a government service?
No, KisanDostAI is not a government service. It is an academic research and development project created as a final year project by Computer Science students at University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Peshawar, Pakistan. The project was developed under the supervision of faculty members with the goal of addressing real agricultural challenges in Pakistan through innovative AI technology. While it's an independent university initiative, we aim to collaborate with government agricultural departments in the future.
How accurate is the information provided?
The information provided by KisanDostAI is based on verified agricultural research, best practices from Pakistan's agricultural universities, and real-time data from reliable sources. Our AI system is trained on extensive agricultural datasets specific to Pakistani farming conditions, climate patterns, and crop varieties. We continuously update and improve our knowledge base to ensure farmers receive accurate, relevant, and actionable advice tailored to local farming conditions.
Can I use it from anywhere in Pakistan?
Yes, KisanDostAI is accessible from anywhere in Pakistan where you have mobile network coverage. Since it operates through standard GSM voice calls rather than internet connectivity, you can use the service from remote rural areas, mountainous regions, or any location with basic mobile phone signal. The service works with all major Pakistani telecom operators including Jazz, Zong, Telenor, and Ufone.
What are the call charges?
While the KisanDostAI service itself is completely free, standard mobile call charges from your telecom operator will apply. These are the same rates you pay for regular voice calls. We recommend using call packages or off-peak hours to minimize costs. We're actively working with telecom operators to potentially offer toll-free or subsidized calling options for farmers in the future to make the service even more accessible.
